Overview

Impulse Inductors are essential components in high-voltage impulse testing circuits, controlling the shape and duration of the impulse waveform. Carona Power produces precision air-cored inductors engineered for accurate lightning impulse, switching impulse, and oscillatory impulse applications. Designed for robust performance, our inductors ensure repeatable, reliable high voltage testing of transformers, arresters, GIS, and other high voltage equipment.

Key Features

  • Air-cored design for minimal saturation
  • Precise inductance control for waveform shaping
  • Multiple tapping options for waveform tuning
  • Designed for high impulse energy environments
  • High mechanical stability for repeat testing
