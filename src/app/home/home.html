About Carona Power

Carona Power Electrical Test Systems is an engineering firm based in Chikkaballapur, near Bangalore. We specialize in High Voltage Impulse Generator service, Repair, Refurbishment, and Spares Support.

With deep industry experience in impulse voltage and current testing systems, Carona Power supports customers who depend on reliable operation of High Voltage Test Equipment for:

  • Routine Testing
  • Type Testing
  • R&D Applications

We are committed to supporting High-Voltage Laboratories and Industrial facilities in achieving safe, compliant, and reliable impulse testing operations.
