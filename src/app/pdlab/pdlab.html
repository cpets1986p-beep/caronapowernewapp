Overview

Carona Power Electrical Test Systems supports organizations in constructing Partial Discharge (PD) Laboratories for advanced non-destructive testing of high voltage equipment.

Partial Discharge testing is essential for detecting insulation weaknesses and internal defects in electrical equipment. A properly designed PD Lab ensures accurate measurement, low-noise performance, and compliance with international testing standards.

Supporting Reliable Non-Destructive Testing Facilities

  • Routine PD testing
  • Type testing and compliance verification
  • R&D and insulation development
  • Condition monitoring and quality assurance

PD Lab Design & Engineering Support Includes:

  • PD laboratory layout planning
  • Grounding and shielding techniques
  • Noise suppression and interference control
  • Integration of PD measurement systems
  • Support for commissioning and safe operation practices
